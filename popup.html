We support almost all math functions:

  • Binary operations: plus (+), minus (-), multiply(*), divide(/), power(^)
  • Trigonometry functions: sin(x),cos(x),tan(x)
  • Complex numbers: a+b*i
  • Logarithms: log(x),ln(x)
  • Constants: PI, e
  • and more... Example: e^(sin(PI)) + e^(cos(PI)) = 1.368
